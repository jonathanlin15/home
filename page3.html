Reactions: What's Causing it:
Anger
  • Random acts of police violence that go unpunished causing citizens to lead non-peaceful protests
  • Stereotypes can cause stigma against the black community and also many other races
Fear
  • When people of colour are treated differently and judged just based of their skin colour
Frustration
  • When someone close to you or know, is discriminated because of their race
  • No matter how much the black community voice their opinions, change is very slow
Hopelessness
  • If you see people, or yourself getting discriminated against over and over again

Ways to Cope:
  • Build a strong support network

  • Utilize your beliefs as a way to cope with stress

  • Having a positive cultural identity and strong sense of self

  • Make Positive reinterpretation of negative thoughts and reframe negative situations with a three step process
    1. Identify negative feelings
    2. Perform a reality check
    3. Make a positive reinterpretation

  • Become involved in social action
