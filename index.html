Racism and Antiracism

rac·ism

/ˈrāˌsizəm/
noun

prejudice, discrimination, or antagonism directed against a person or people on the basis of their membership of a particular racial or ethnic group, typically one that is a minority or marginalized.

Racism is a huge thing in our world nowadays. Everyone deserves to be treated equally. No one should be judged by their skin colour or how they look. In my website, I will be going over some tips/advice we can use to educate and also protect ourselves against racism.
