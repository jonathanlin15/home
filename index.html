rac·ism

/ˈrāˌsizəm/

noun

prejudice, discrimination, or antagonism directed against a person or people on the basis of their membership of a particular racial or ethnic group, typically one that is a minority or marginalized.

Although our society has changed a great deal from the past, racism is still a very real issue in our lives that many people face. Everyone deserves to be treated equally. No one should be judged by the colour of their skin or how they look. In my website, I will be going over some tips/advice we can use to educate ourselves and also protect ourselves against racism.

Though racism against any race is important to know about, in my website, I will be mainly talking about racism against the Black Community
